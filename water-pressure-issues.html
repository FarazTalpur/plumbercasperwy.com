Common Water Pressure Problems

Understanding the potential culprits behind water pressure issues can help in addressing them effectively:

  • Clogged Pipes : Accumulations of debris, mineral deposits, or rust within pipes can obstruct water flow.
  • Faulty Pressure Regulator : A malfunctioning regulator can result in pressure that is too low or too high.
  • Leaks : Hidden leaks in the plumbing system can lead to decreased water pressure.
  • Municipal Supply Issues : Sometimes, the problem stems from the main water supply.

Low pressure in the shower may be due to a clogged showerhead, issues with the pressure regulator, or plumbing system blockages. Clean the showerhead and check the pressure regulator.
